CCA

As a member of Kent Ridge secondary school's basketball club from 2018 to 2022. I represented the school in the South zone 'C' Divison basketball championship in 2018 and 2019 and the South zone 'B' Division basketball championship in 2022. I was also the training IC, where I was in charge of helping to train my teammates and juniors in their fundamentals and skills.

In 2019, I took part in an Overseas learning journey to Johor Bahru, Malaysia. Organized by the basketball club where I was involved in putting up a performance for the elderly of a Nursing Home and also played against a few Malaysian basketball teams.
